How this score works

This quiz looks at four practice areas that strongly influence Soil Organic Carbon (SOC) in Indian cropping systems: crop rotation and diversity, tillage intensity, residue management, and irrigation practices. Each answer is scored 0–3, for a maximum of 12 points. Your result gives a directional picture of where your farm stands and which one or two changes are likely to have the biggest impact — it is not a substitute for an on-site soil test or carbon eligibility assessment.
