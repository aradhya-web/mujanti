Carbon Credits Explained

Your Soil Is a Carbon Sink.
It Should Pay You.

When farmers adopt reduced tillage, cover cropping, agroforestry, or composting, their soil absorbs CO₂. Companies worldwide pay for this sequestration to meet climate targets.

01

Eligibility Assessment

We evaluate your land, farming practices and region to determine carbon credit potential and income per hectare.

02

Baseline Measurement

Soil testing and satellite data establish your carbon baseline — the foundation for registry submission.

03

Registry Enrollment

We register your project under Verra VCS or Gold Standard in your FPO's name, protecting your credits legally.

04

Annual Verification & Payment

Credits verified yearly. We connect you to buyers and ensure income flows directly to your FPO account.
