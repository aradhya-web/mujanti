FAQ

Common Questions Answered

Everything you need to know before getting started with FPO formation or carbon credits.

How long does FPO registration take? ▼
With all documents in order, MCA registration for a Farmer Producer Company typically takes 4–8 weeks. Our team handles the entire process, including DIN procurement, name approval, and ROC filing, so you don't have to navigate it alone.
What is the minimum number of farmers to form an FPO? ▼
A Farmer Producer Company under the Companies Act 2013 requires a minimum of 10 farmer members and 2 directors. For NABARD equity grant eligibility, a minimum of 300 members (500 in hilly areas) is preferred, but formation can begin with fewer.
Does my farm qualify for carbon credits? ▼
Most Indian farmland practising or willing to adopt reduced tillage, cover cropping, agroforestry, or composting is eligible. A minimum contiguous area of 100–200 hectares aggregated across FPO members is generally required for Verra VCS registration. We do a free eligibility assessment.
How much do your services cost? ▼
FPO formation and compliance services are priced as fixed packages starting from ₹35,000. Carbon credit consulting operates on a success-fee model — we charge no upfront fees and earn only when your FPO earns. Digital and media services are quoted per project scope.
Which government schemes can FPOs access? ▼
Registered FPOs can access SFAC equity grants (up to ₹15 lakh), NABARD refinance and capital grants, PM-FME processing subsidies, PM-KISAN benefits for member farmers, state-level FPO promotion schemes, and APEDA export support. We identify and apply for all applicable schemes on your behalf.
What is the carbon credit payment timeline? ▼
From application to first credit issuance typically takes 6–12 months, covering baseline surveys, project documentation, registry enrollment and third-party verification. After that, credits are issued and sold annually. Payments go directly into the FPO's bank account.
Do you produce content in regional languages? ▼
Yes. Our media and training content is produced in Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Odia, and Bengali on request. All farmer-facing digital tools (apps, bots, portals) are also built with regional language support as standard.
Can you help an existing FPO, not just new formations? ▼
Absolutely. A large portion of our work is with existing FPOs — fixing compliance gaps, enrolling them in carbon programs, building their digital presence, and producing impact media that helps them attract grants and CSR funding. We audit your current situation in the free consultation call.
